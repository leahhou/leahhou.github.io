Marketplace_

Vinyl Marketplace is an eCommerce marketplace to buy and sell vinyl records. Unlike traditional music market, Vinly Marketplace is tailored to provide an unique shopping experience for the vinyl collector that allow users to directly communicate on the platform.
Fully tested and feasible to scale, Vinyl marketplace have main features including search feature, single log in system, secured 3rd party payment handling.
Skills: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, Heroku, Stripe API.
